## Welcome to the new Tibcert

After months of planning and collaboration, we are excited to unveil our new website and digital identity. This transition is more than just a visual update; it represents our commitment to enhancing the digital resilience of our community.

## What's New?

- **Community Reports**: A streamlined system to deliver critical security insights.
- **Enhanced Services**: Clearer paths for incident response and security training.
- **Collaborative Network**: A new framework for organizations to join our coalition.

We believe that a secure digital future for the Tibetan community is built on trust, transparency, and collaboration. Thank you for joining us on this journey.
