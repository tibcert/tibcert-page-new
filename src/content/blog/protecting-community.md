## Resilience Through Collaboration

In an era where digital threats are increasingly sophisticated, the Tibetan community faces unique challenges. From targeted phishing attacks to disinformation campaigns, our digital space is a frontline for community safety.

## The Power of Shared Intelligence

At Tibcert, we believe that *information sharing* is our greatest defense. By working together through the Traffic Light Protocol (TLP), we can identify threats early and protect our organizations before an incident occurs.

### Key Takeaways

1. **Stay Vigilant**: Always verify the source of digital communications.
2. **Report Early**: Prompt reporting of an incident can prevent it from spreading.
3. **Invest in Training**: Knowledge is the first line of defense.

Building digital resilience is a collective effort. Together, we can ensure a safer digital future for all Tibetans.
